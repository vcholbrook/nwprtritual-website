What is NWPRT Ritual?

NWPRT Ritual is a 4-day immersive wellness retreat in Newport Beach, California, designed exclusively for women who are ready to understand their bodies at the deepest biological level.

This isn’t a spa weekend or a conference. It’s a full reset, grounded in your DNA, your bloodwork, your wearable data, and the expertise of functional medicine practitioners who actually listen.

You’ll rotate through four expert-led pods, participate in coastal activities that restore your nervous system, and leave Sunday with a 90-day personalized roadmap that finally makes sense for your body.

Why Newport

Newport Beach isn’t just a setting. It’s a state of mind. One of the world’s great small boat harbors. Eight miles of Pacific coastline. The kind of place that makes slowing down feel like the most natural thing in the world.

Newport Harbor

One of the finest small boat harbors in the world. 9,000+ vessels. The yacht dinner backdrop that makes Saturday evening unforgettable.

Crystal Cove

Historic 1920s coastal cottages, 3.2 miles of natural seashore. Once the Hollywood elite getaway. Still magical.

Pacific Coast Highway

The iconic coastal ride. Saturday morning on bikes. Salt air, ocean views, 24 women finding their current.

Fashion Island

Open-air luxury at the heart of Newport. The backdrop for the lifestyle you’ve earned and are learning to inhabit fully.

Balboa Island

2.6 miles of waterfront charm. Boutiques, dining, the ferry. The kind of afternoon that makes everything feel possible.

The Pacific

Dolphins. Gray whales. 8 miles of coastline. A constant reminder: tides shift. So do we.
